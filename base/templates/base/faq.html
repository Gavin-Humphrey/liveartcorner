Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is LIVE-ART-CORNER?

LIVE-ART-CORNER is your one-stop gallery for discovering and acquiring amazing art. It's a platform where users can buy artworks and custom services provided by certified artists.

Who are the artists featured on LIVE-ART-CORNER?

Our platform features certified artists who have been carefully selected based on their portfolio, expertise, and the quality of their work.

Purchasing Art

How do I purchase an artwork?

To purchase an artwork, simply browse our collection, select the piece you wish to buy, and follow the checkout process to complete your purchase.

Can I request a custom artwork?

Yes, you can request custom artworks by contacting the artist directly through their profile or by using the custom services section on our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options.

Shipping and Delivery

How is the artwork shipped?

Artworks are carefully packaged and shipped using reliable courier services to ensure they arrive safely at your doorstep.

What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs vary depending on the size and weight of the artwork, as well as the destination. Shipping fees will be calculated during the checkout process.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on the chosen delivery option and the shipping destination. Typically, it takes between 7 to 14 business days for your artwork to arrive.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the artwork is in its original condition.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order details, and they will guide you through the process.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned artwork. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the contact form on our website, or by emailing us at liveartcorner01@gmail.com.
