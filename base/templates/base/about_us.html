About Us

Welcome to LIVE-ART-CORNER! We are more than just an online gallery; we are a vibrant community where art meets passion. Our platform serves as a bridge between talented, certified artists and art enthusiasts who appreciate unique, high-quality artworks.

Our Mission

At LIVE-ART-CORNER, our mission is to create a space where art can be celebrated and discovered effortlessly. We strive to bring the finest and most diverse collection of artworks right to your fingertips. Whether you're looking for a breathtaking painting to adorn your living room, a bespoke piece crafted by a talented artist, or an opportunity to support emerging talents, we are here to make your art journey seamless and enjoyable.

What We Offer

Our platform features a wide range of art pieces from various genres and styles, curated to suit every taste. We offer:

  • Unique Artworks: Browse through an extensive collection of original artworks, including paintings, sculptures, and digital art, all created by certified artists.
  • Custom Art Services: Looking for something truly one-of-a-kind? Connect with our artists to commission custom artworks tailored to your specific preferences.
  • Secure Transactions: Enjoy a hassle-free purchasing experience with our secure payment methods and reliable shipping services.
  • Artist Profiles: Learn more about the artists behind the artworks, including their portfolios, biographies, and creative processes.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries or issues, ensuring your experience with us is smooth and satisfying.

Our Values

We are driven by a commitment to excellence and integrity. Our core values include:

  • Quality: We prioritize high standards in the selection and presentation of artworks, ensuring that every piece on our platform meets our stringent quality criteria.
  • Transparency: We believe in clear and honest communication with both our artists and customers, providing detailed information about artworks and transactions.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek to enhance our platform and services, embracing new technologies and trends to offer a cutting-edge art experience.
  • Community: We value the connections we build within the art community and strive to support and uplift artists while fostering a passionate and engaged audience.

Join Us

Whether you're an art enthusiast searching for your next masterpiece or an artist looking to showcase your work, we invite you to join the LIVE-ART-CORNER family. Explore our platform, connect with like-minded individuals, and discover the beauty and creativity that our artists bring to life.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to sharing the world of art with you!
