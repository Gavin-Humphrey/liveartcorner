Our Services

At LIVE-ART-CORNER, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of services to meet all your art needs. Our offerings include:

Custom Artwork Commissions

Looking for something truly unique? Our platform allows you to commission custom artworks tailored to your specific preferences. If a client books for a custom art service, a date will be scheduled based on available date/time slots in the artist's custom art service portal. During this scheduled session, the artist will create a personalized artwork – be it an individual portrait, family portrait, landscape art, etc., based on your order – via a webcam. The completed artwork will then be delivered to your doorstep using a reliable delivery service. Please note that only registered customers can access custom art commissions. Simply browse through artist profiles, choose the one that resonates with your style, and collaborate directly with them to create a bespoke piece.

Art Consultations

Not sure which artwork would best complement your space or collection? Our art consultation service is designed to assist you. Our experienced art consultants can provide personalized advice and recommendations based on your tastes, space, and budget. Whether you need help selecting a single piece or curating an entire collection, we are here to ensure you make the perfect choice.

Artist Collaborations

We believe in the power of collaboration. Our platform facilitates connections between artists and clients for unique projects. Whether you are looking to create a large-scale mural, a series of artworks for a corporate space, or a special event installation, our artists are ready to collaborate with you to make your vision a reality. Explore our artist profiles and initiate a collaboration to start your creative journey.

Art Workshops and Events

At LIVE-ART-CORNER, we offer various workshops and events aimed at fostering creativity and artistic skills. From painting classes to sculpture workshops, our events cater to all skill levels and interests. Join our workshops to learn new techniques, get inspired, and connect with other art enthusiasts. Keep an eye on our event calendar for upcoming workshops and special events.

Virtual Art Exhibitions

Experience art from the comfort of your home with our virtual art exhibitions. Our platform features curated online exhibitions showcasing the works of talented artists. Explore diverse collections, read artist statements, and even purchase artworks directly from the exhibition. Stay tuned for our latest virtual exhibitions and discover new art from around the world.

Art Sales and Rentals

Whether you are looking to buy or rent art, LIVE-ART-CORNER has got you covered. Browse our extensive collection of original artworks available for sale. If you are not ready to commit to a purchase, consider our art rental service. Renting art allows you to enjoy different pieces over time and find the perfect fit for your space before making a purchase.

Customer Support

Our dedicated customer support team is always here to assist you. From inquiries about our services to support with your orders, we are committed to providing excellent service and ensuring your satisfaction. Contact us with any questions or concerns, and we will be happy to help.

Explore our services and let LIVE-ART-CORNER be your trusted partner in the world of art. We are here to inspire, create, and connect through the power of art.
